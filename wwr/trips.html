Featured Expeditions

Half-Day Fun Run

Perfect for families and beginners! This 3-hour trip covers 5 miles of scenic river with Class I and II rapids. Enjoy a snack break on a sandy beach and plenty of swimming opportunities.

Full-Day Adventure

Our most popular trip. Experience 12 miles of thrill with Class III and IV rapids. Lunch is included on the riverbank. Great for groups of friends looking for an adrenaline rush.

Multi-Day Expedition

Disconnect from the world on this 3-day journey deep into the canyon. Camp under the stars, enjoy gourmet meals cooked by guides, and conquer the legendary "Thunder" rapids.
